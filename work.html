JACI STICKROD

Software Engineering Intern at Lockheed Martin

At Lockheed, I wore many hats. I mainly focused on the CCOR instrument model for the GOES-U simulation. This included looking through documentation provided by the client and implementing the desired functionality. I had various small tooling projects to update the simulation as well. Python and C++ were the main languages that I used. Working on the GOES-U simulation is a point of pride for me, the GOES-U satellite is a global weather satellite that most weather information comes from. I met great people at Lockheed and am grateful for my experience there.

Intern at Digicomm

I worked for Digicomm International, a small telecommunications company, from May 2019 to August 2019. There I was responsible for many administrative tasks like answering phones, supporting the sales and accounting teams and assisting in various customer service duties. My main project at Digicomm was a CRM database used to capture customer contact information. I cleaned data and helped design a database with the lead software engineer and other executives. I was exposed to the process of preparing data for a database and creating a relational database.
